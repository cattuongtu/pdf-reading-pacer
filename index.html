How To Start Pacing Your PDF:

Step by Step

1. Enter the link of your desired PDF, then click on the "Show PDF" button. Make sure the PDF that appears is the correct one.

2. Click on the "Pacer Mode" button to display the text that will be paced for you soon.

3. Select your desired reading speed.

4. Set the start and end page for your reading session and click "Save".

5. Select your desired highlight color. (Default is yellow).

6. Click the "Start Pacing Button" to start your session!
